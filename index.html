Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

For beginners, it's about taking the first steps to learn, explore, and create. With curiosity and dreams, they open doors to endless possibilities.

Elementary School

In elementary school, children learn, play, and explore the world around them. Their curiosity drives them to create and dream, forming the foundation of their education.

Middle (10-16) Years

Middle school is a time to grow, explore, and dream bigger. Students learn to balance creativity with new responsibilities, developing their potential.
